Our Heritage

At Munambam, quality is not just a standard; it is our foundation. We combine the authenticity of Kerala's heritage seafood with modern, uncompromising quality standards.

Our Quality Assurance Protocol

  • Direct Sourcing: We bypass middle-men, sourcing our catch directly from traditional day-boats at Munambam Harbour to ensure peak freshness.
  • Natural Processing: Our drying and pickling techniques rely on time-honored artisanal methods, completely eliminating artificial colors, synthetic flavors, and chemical preservatives.
